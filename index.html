About Me

Hello! I’m Rishab, a dedicated student at Vivekanand Education Society's Institute of Technology (VSIT) with a strong passion for web development and software engineering. Recently, I completed a Front-End Developer certification from Meta, where I gained advanced knowledge in creating dynamic, responsive, and user-friendly web applications. Through this certification, I’ve honed my skills in technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, along with tools like Jest for testing and Tailwind CSS for efficient styling.

Beyond academics, I am constantly working on personal projects to apply and refine my front-end development skills. I’m excited to start building more real-world applications that not only solve problems but also provide seamless user experiences. As I continue to grow in the field, my goal is to contribute to innovative projects and collaborate with other developers to create impactful digital solutions.
